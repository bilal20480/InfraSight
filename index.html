Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn credits?

10 credits per verified report, 5 per upvoted issue.

What can I redeem credits for?

Vouchers, community recognition, or priority for your reports.

How to access dashboards?

Login with approved government email ID.

Is the data exportable?

Yes, all reports and analytics can be exported in multiple formats.
